FAIR* Reviews

FAIR* Reviews aim to leverage the full potential of scientific reviews by exposing them publicly to the scientific community. Our goal is to provide reviewers with the proper means to expose their work on the web, making them available and consumable by different means. FAIR* Reviews are semantically-rich and web-first digital objects, which follow the Linked Open Data initiative and are aligned with the FAIR data principles

Vocabulary

The FAIR* Reviews vocabulary defines a set of classes, properties and axioms, for describing research reviews as semantic objects, reusing standard existing vocabularies using ontology engineering techniques.

Linked Data services

FAIR* Reviews offers a platfomr in which reviews can be published as Linked Data, supporting, among others, the generation of permanent identifiers and content negotiation for resources. Data about reviews will be represented as JSON-LD, supporting data queries both by means of an SPARQL endpoint and JSON APIs.

Portal

The FAIR* Reviews portal allows to create, edit and fully manage reviews, integrating 3rd party providers such as ORCID or OpenCitations for enabling more trustable reviews.
